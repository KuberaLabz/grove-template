Our Story

Food that
knows where
it came from.

GROVE opened in 2018 with one rule: nothing on the plate travels more than 100 miles to get there. What started as a constraint became a philosophy — and eventually, a kitchen connected to the land in a way most restaurants aren't.

Head chef Mateo Ruiz spent seven years in the Hudson Valley farming before he ever cooked professionally. That background shows in every dish. He doesn't just source from farms. He grows on them.
